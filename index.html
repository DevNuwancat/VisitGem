Our Story

Explore the charm of Gem City Project

Sabaragamuwa is known for its lush landscapes, rich biodiversity, and cultural heritage. And full of natural beauty and travel opportunities such as Forests ,waterfalls gem-mining, national parks ,culture and religious places. Visit gemcity programme is started to expose the natural beauty of Sabaragamuwa to the whole world based on eco tourism concept.

Coordination with the recognised institutions that Provide high level of hospitality including food and beverage, accommodation , health and sanitary as well as the security for travellers is the vision of visit gemcity team. Distributing to Enhance the economy of country and forming new business opportunities for srilankans is the one of objectives of the team
